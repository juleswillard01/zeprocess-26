# MEGA QUIXAI - Business Model & Stratégie de Monétisation

**Document:** Business Model Autonome - Agents IA Francophones
**Niche:** Coaching Séduction/Développement Personnel Masculin (20-35 ans)
**Date:** 2026-03-14
**Statut:** Version 1.0 - Strategy Framework

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

MEGA QUIXAI est un système de 3 agents IA autonomes (Lead Acquisition, Séduction, Closing) conçu pour générer du revenu récurrent en vendant du coaching personnalisé IA + formations + communauté premium à des hommes francophones introvertis/timides cherchant à améliorer leur vie amoureuse.

**Proposition de valeur:**
- Coaching IA personnalisé 24/7 (par rapport à coaching humain 1x/semaine)
- Formations vidéo immersives basées sur DDP Garçonnière (content propriétaire)
- Communauté privée d'accountability + support pair
- Escalade vers coaching humain pour clients haute valeur

**Modèle de revenu:** Tiered SaaS + Produits informationnels
- Tier 1 (Formation seule): 97-197€
- Tier 2 (Formation + Coaching IA): 497€
- Tier 3 (Coaching High-Ticket humain): 1500-3000€/mois
- Upsells: Spécialités (approche, texting, sortir de friendzone), masterclasses

**Ambition financière (12 mois):**
- Mois 3: 5K€ ARR (5-10 clients actifs)
- Mois 6: 50K€ ARR (40-60 clients actifs)
- Mois 12: 200K€ ARR (150-200 clients actifs)

## 1. OFFRE PRODUIT (PRODUCT-MARKET FIT)

### 1.1 Architecture Produit - Tiered System

#### TIER 1: "Formation Express" (Self-Service)
**Prix:** 97€ (one-time) ou 29€/mois (subscription)
**Contenu:**
- 6-8 modules vidéo DDP Garçonnière (regroupés par thème: aborder, conversation, créer attraction)
- Workbook PDF (50 pages): exercices de mindset, checklists, scripts d'approche
- Accès 6 mois à Discord (communauté gratuit, pas de modération premium)
- Email séquence (14 emails) avec daily tips

**Métrique de succès:**
- Téléchargement workbook = engagement proxy
- 30% ouverture email

**Qui achète:** Explorateurs, penny-pinchers, souhaitent tester la niche
**LTV estimé:** 50-80€ (certains upgraderont après test)

---

#### TIER 2: "Coaching IA Complet" (Core Product)
**Prix:** 497€/mois (ou 4970€/an, -17% discount)
**Inclus:**
- Accès illimité à l'agent "Séduction" (coaching personnalisé IA)
  - Analyse de profils de matchs avec recommandations
  - Critiques de messages avant envoi
  - Analyse vidéo d'approches (si fourni)
  - Plans d'action personnalisés basés sur profil comportemental
- Accès illimité à l'agent "Closing" pour conversion des prospects
- Discord premium:
  - Salon privé par thème (aborder, texting, relation long-terme)
  - Weekly group coaching (30min, animé par agent IA + humain)
  - Accountability buddies matching
- Templates & frameworks:
  - 50 approches contextualisées (bar, app, rue, travail)
  - 100+ scripts de conversation (ouverture, qualification, transition sexuelle)
  - Analyse comportementale personnalisée (MBTI-like framework)
- Rapports mensuels: progression, insights, recommandations suivantes
- Priorité support (réponse <2h sur questions)

**Métrique de succès:**
- Churn <8%/mois (rétention >92%)
- 60% engagement actif (au moins 3 interactions/semaine)
- 40% conversion vers high-ticket dans les 3 mois

**Qui achète:** Early adopters sérieux, prêts à payer pour résultats, veulent accompagnement
**LTV estimé:** 2000-2500€ (4 mois avg retention)

---

#### TIER 3: "High-Ticket Coaching Humain" (Premium)
**Prix:** 1500-3000€/mois (6 ou 12 mois commitment)
**Inclus:**
- Tout de Tier 2 (Coaching IA + Discord)
- 2x appels 1:1 humain/mois (30-45min) avec coach expérimenté
- Plan d'action mensuel personalisé + ajustement hebdomadaire (Slack)
- Intro-trading direct vers partenaires/communauté d'influence
- Évaluation vidéo approche réelle + feedback détaillé
- Garantie de résultats: "Si pas de date qualifiée en 3 mois, 50% remboursé"

**Métrique de succès:**
- 0% churn (locked in 6-12 mois)
- 70% satisfaction NPS
- 80% résultats mesurables (dates qualifiées, relations)

**Qui achète:** High-intent, souhaitent résultats garantis, ont budget limité mais prêts à investir
**LTV estimé:** 9000-18000€ (6-12 mois)

### 2.2 Stratégie de Conversion

#### 1. Anchor Pricing
**Formation Express (97€) = anchor** pour perceived value
- Clients voient 97€ comme "entrée", puis 497€ comme "investissement sérieux" (5x)
- Psychologie: 497€ vs 1497€ seem close, mais 97€ to 497€ = clear jump

#### 2. Time-Limited Discounts
- **Launch (Mois 1-2):** 30% off Tier 2 (réduction à 347€/mois) pour early adopters
- **Annual Prepay:** -17% discount (4970€ vs 6x497€ = 2982€)
- **Referral:** 20% off si ami parrainé achète Tier 2

#### 3. Bundling Strategy
- Formation Express + Masterclass (3 pass) = 127€ (-20%)
- Tier 2 + Master Spécialité = 507€ (-17%, vs 497+97)

#### 4. Psychological Pricing
- 497€/mois (not 500€): anchoring bas
- 4970€/an (not 5000€): même effet
- 1500€ (not 1495€): premium tier = exact number, confidence

### 2.3 Payment Plans & Friction Reduction

## 3. FUNNEL DESIGN (Customer Journey)

### 3.1 Full-Funnel Workflow

```
AWARENESS (Free) → CONSIDERATION (Lead Magnet) → CONVERSION (Sales) → RETENTION → UPSELL
```

---

### 3.2 AWARENESS STAGE (Agent Lead Acquisition)

**Channel 1: Instagram Organic Content**
- Agent scrapes trending posts in niche (hashtags: #coachingseduction, #développementpersonnel, #confiance)
- Posts 5-7x/week (mix of):
  - Educational (40%): "5 erreurs en approche", "Pourquoi ta bio dating app sucks"
  - Story-driven (30%): Success stories anonyme, transformations clients
  - Controversy/hook (20%): Counterintuitive takes ("Dating apps killed your confidence")
  - CTAs (10%): "Comment améliorer ta vie amoureuse? Lien en bio"
- Target: 2000-5000 followers par mois (first 6 mois)
- Engagement target: 3-5% (comments, saves, shares)

**Channel 2: Scraping Ciblé (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook Groups)**
- Agent Lead Acq identifie profils matching:
  - Age 20-35, langue FR
  - Intérêts: coaching, self-help, dating, mindset
  - Signaux: posts about loneliness, dating struggles, self-improvement
- Approche soft DM: "Salut [Name], j'ai vu ton post sur [topic]. Je fais du coaching dans ce domaine, tu as 2min pour discuter?" (30% response rate target)
- Qualifier: est-ce qu'ils sought coaching avant? Budget? Timeline?

**Channel 3: SEO & Content Marketing**
- Blog posts (3-4 articles/mois) sur: "Comment aborder une fille en 2026", "Profil dating app parfait", etc.
- Target: 1000-2000 organic visitors/mois (long term)
- Optimisation: IA génère content, agent posts, humain optimise SEO

---

### 3.3 CONSIDERATION STAGE (Lead Magnet & Email)

**Lead Magnet:** "Guide Complet: Comment Aborder une Fille (5 contextes différents)"
- Format: PDF 20 pages + video 12min (agent Séduction explique framework)
- Requirement: email pour download
- Target: 5-10% of traffic converts (100 visitors = 5-10 leads/jour)

**Email Sequence (7 days):**
```
Day 0: Welcome + PDF Link + Expectation Setting
Day 1: Video Tutorial (agent explique approche)
Day 2: Common Mistakes (social proof: what 80% do wrong)
Day 3: Advanced Framework (teaser of formations)
Day 4: Social Proof (success stories)
Day 5: Soft CTA (formation express 97€, limited time)
Day 6: FOMO (last chance email)
Day 7: Offer Expires + Upsell to 497€ Tier
```

**Conversion target:** 5-8% click CTA, 1-2% conversion to purchase

---

### 3.4 CONVERSION STAGE (Sales Funnel)

#### Micro-Conversion 1: Formation Express (97€)
**Trigger:** Landed on sales page from email or organic Instagram
**Agent Séduction (automation):**
1. Collect form: email, age, biggest challenge in dating
2. Show AI persona match (e.g., "You're an Ambivert Cautious type - here's your personalized path")
3. Show 3-video carousel (benefits, success story, framework overview)
4. CTA: "Get Formation Express" (97€, 14-day guarantee, refund policy)
5. Post-purchase: Welcome email, Discord invite, first video access

**Micro-Conversion 2: Upgrade to Tier 2 (497€)
**Trigger:** Watched 3+ videos or completed 1 workbook exercise
**Agent Séduction:**
1. Send in-app notification: "You're making progress! Next step: personalized coaching"
2. Show comparison (Formation vs Coaching IA) with social proof
3. Personalized offer: "Based on your challenges ([identified from form]), Coaching IA works for X% of people like you"
4. CTA: "Start Coaching IA Trial" (7-day free trial OR first month 197€ instead of 497€)
5. Conversion target: 15-20% of Formation buyers upgrade in 30 days

---

#### Conversion: Tier 2 to High-Ticket (1500€/mois)
**Trigger:** Coaching IA client + 3+ weeks active usage + achieved 1 milestone (date, improve text game, etc.)
**Agent Closing + Human:**
1. Agent flags: "This client is high-intent, approaching milestone"
2. Human reviews, qualifies: budget check, readiness for commitment
3. Offer: "You're making progress with IA. Want to accelerate with 1:1 coaching?"
4. Sales call (15min) with coach: address objections, demo value
5. Conversion target: 10-15% of Tier 2 clients → High-Ticket

#### Channel 3: Paid Ads (Phase 2, Month 6+)

- **CAC per Formation (ads):** 2€ / 0.05 = 40€
- **Tier 2 upgrade (20%):** 40€ / 0.20 = 200€ initial, but LTV covers

**Blended CAC (ads):** ~45€ (paid only), ~5€ blended with organic

### 4.2 Lifetime Value (LTV)

#### LTV by Cohort

**Cohort A: Formation Express Only**
```
Avg Purchase: 97€
Avg Retention: 1 purchase (no subscription)
Upsell Rate: 20% to Tier 2 later
Upsell Value: 497€/mth × 4mth avg = 1988€

LTV = (97€ × 0.8) + (1988€ × 0.2) = 77.6€ + 397.6€ = 475€
```

**Cohort B: Tier 2 (Coaching IA)**
```
Entry Price: 497€/month
Avg Retention: 4 months (churn 8%/mth)
Churn Curve: M1 97%, M2 94%, M3 90%, M4 85%, M5+ 0% (downgrade)
LTV = 497€ × (0.97 + 0.94 + 0.90 + 0.85) = 497€ × 3.66 = 1818€

Add upsells (specialize: 30% take 27€/mth):
  Addon LTV = 27€ × 0.30 × 3.66 = 30€
Add referrals (20% bring 1 friend, 20% conversion):
  Referral LTV = (1 × 497€ × 0.20 × 0.20 × 4) = 79€

TOTAL LTV = 1818€ + 30€ + 79€ = 1927€
```

**Cohort C: High-Ticket Coaching (1500€/mth)**
```
Entry Price: 1500€/month
Avg Retention: 9 months (6-month lock-in, then 8/10 churn)
Churn Curve: M1-M6 100%, M7 80%, M8 70%, M9 60%

LTV = 1500€ × (6 + 0.80 + 0.70 + 0.60) = 1500€ × 8.1 = 12150€

Add upsells (minimal, mostly in product):
  Addon LTV = 50€ (small extras)

Add referrals (premium clients refer):
  Referral LTV = (0.5 referral × 1500€ × 3mth avg) = 2250€

TOTAL LTV = 12150€ + 50€ + 2250€ = 14450€
```

## 12. APPENDIX: Glossary & References

### Glossary
- **Formation Express:** Entry-level product (97€ one-time or 29€/month)
- **Tier 2 / Coaching IA:** Core product (497€/month, IA-driven coaching)
- **Tier 3 / High-Ticket:** Premium product (1500-3000€/month, human coaching + IA)
- **CAC:** Customer Acquisition Cost (avg cost to acquire one customer)
- **LTV:** Lifetime Value (total revenue from customer minus COGS over lifetime)
- **Churn:** % of customers leaving per month
- **ARPU:** Average Revenue Per User (blended)
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score (loyalty metric, -100 to +100)
- **ROAS:** Return on Ad Spend (revenue / ad spend)

### Key Financial Terms
- **Gross Margin:** (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
- **Contribution Margin:** Revenue after variable costs
- **Operating Margin:** (Revenue - all costs) / Revenue
- **Break-even:** Revenue where Operating Margin = 0

### References & Tools
- **Analytics:** Mixpanel, Segment, Amplitude
- **Email:** Brevo, ConvertKit
- **CRM:** Lemlist, HubSpot, Pipedrive
- **Payments:** Stripe, Lemonsqueezy
- **Infrastructure:** Render, Fly.io, Railway
- **Discord:** Discord.py, discord-interactions
